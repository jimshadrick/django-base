Privacy Policy

Effective Date: {% now "F d, Y" %}

1. Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal data when you use our website and services. Our approach is guided by data minimization: we collect and retain only the information necessary to operate the application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the personal information necessary to authenticate and manage your account. This typically includes an email address for account authentication and credentials (stored securely). We do not require or collect additional personal profile information unless you voluntarily provide it through the application.

We may process limited technical information for security and operational purposes, such as temporary server logs and error or performance diagnostics. These data are used strictly for operational purposes, are not used to identify individual users, and are routinely discarded.

We do not intentionally collect personal data such as IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or tracking identifiers for analytics or advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  1. Operate, maintain, and improve the application and our services
  2. Authenticate users and manage accounts
  3. Send essential service-related communications (such as account verification emails)
  4. Maintain the security and integrity of the system
  5. Provide customer support

We do not use personal data for marketing, profiling, or advertising.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information.

Personal data may be processed by service providers strictly as necessary to operate the application (for example, hosting infrastructure or email delivery services). These providers process data only on our behalf and are subject to confidentiality and security obligations.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as an account remains active.

Users may delete their account and associated personal data at any time using the account settings within the application. When an account is deleted:

  1. Personal data associated with the account is permanently removed from the application database
  2. Authentication credentials are invalidated
  3. The user can no longer access the service

We do not retain personal data after account deletion except as described in the section on backups below.

6. Database Backups

To ensure system reliability and recovery in the event of failure, the application database may be backed up periodically.

These backups:

  1. Are stored securely
  2. Are retained for a limited period
  3. Are not accessed for routine operations

If an account is deleted, its data may persist in backups until those backups are automatically overwritten or destroyed according to the backup retention schedule. Backup data is not restored except in the event of system recovery and is protected by the same security measures as live data.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access your personal information
  2. Correct inaccurate information
  3. Delete your account and associated data

These actions can be performed directly through the application. If you require assistance, you may contact us using the information below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@djbase.com.
